Yugarambh


They say, "In every end, there is also a beginning."
We say, "There is nothing such as an end or a beginning, everything is in the form or an endless circle, things just pass from one form to another."
Yugarambh was a remarkable event occasion wherein the charge was passed from the hands of the outgoing council into the hands of the incoming council. Yugarambh was beautifully organized on the 28th of June, 2020 by the Incoming Council, pioneered by DRR Rtr Shraddha Lamkhede.
With the fulfillment of the tenure of our very own IPDRR Rtr Akshay More we hade DRR Rtr Shraddha Lamkhede stepping up as the DRR of RID 3131, on one side we were bidding farewell to a phenomenal council of RIY 2019 - 20 at the same time on the other side we had the enthusiastic council of RIY 2020 - 21 joining in.


STAR : SET TO AUGMENT RESULT


STAR: Set to Augment Reality was our very first event as the "District Executive Council," to be honest all of us were a bit nervous after the announcement of STAR, it was during this event when we as the "District Executive Council," were to be trained to lead a set of more than two thousand Rotaractors of our glorious 3131.
We still remember it was three days to STAR when we got a message from the core to ace this event like a pro, as it was our first event, that message made us a bit more nervous.
This very event marked the beginning of our tenure as council members, we learned a lot in this event, from the minutest detail about the history of Rotary and Rotaract to various important things such as various procedures and protocols.
Now that most of you have experienced at least one district event, you must be aware of the fact that 3131 works as hard as possible during our working hours but when it comes to chilling and bonding no other organization is even near to us, during STAR the bond that we made with other council members was the strongest bond you can ever witness in any professional organization.


KEY : KEEP ENHANCING YOURSELF


Under the standing committee Professional Development, we wanted to give Rotaractors something that would add to their CV and help them build a career.
It is with this thought in mind that we came up with a novel District PD initiative - K.E.Y. ,i.e. Keep Enhancing Yourself.
The world has various opportunities to offer all we have to do is find the Key to it!
District 3131 this year shall attempt to bring variously modules across various disciplines under this initiative and enable Rotaractors to unlock opportunities!
We have already launched our Module 1- Lingua Franca under which we are bringing foreign language courses at a low price. We already have started with 2 chapters of our Module 1 of Lingua France, under which, we have our French and German classes running in full momentum.

Ganpati Bappa Morya


In these 11 days of Ganpati Festival our District PRO's conducted an event to celebrate this festival as one team even during this pandemic.
All our rotaractors were asked to click pictures of themselves while celebrating this festival and put up the same on their stories and tag our district account. A video having all these pictures was put up on YouTube as well.

E-District Fellowship Meet


Every year we organize a fellowship meeting for all the Rotaractors of our District to meet and bind together. We include various activities for all the participants so that through these activities they bond with each other and develop a lifelong connection with each other.
Due to the ongoing pandemic, this year we had to organize our annual District Fellowship Meeting (DFM) virtually with the help of the ZOOM platform, thus calling it E - District Fellowship Meeting (EDFM)
EDFM was phenomenally organized by the Rotaract Club of Pune Camp Pioneers and the Rotaract Club of Indira College on the 14th, 15th, and the 16th of August, 2020. As the first District event, EDFM was a blockbuster that shattered all the records and aced the opportunity.
The overall theme for EDFM was Rise Through Rotaract (abbreviated as RTR) which explains the overall motive of our Rotaract Movement.


A.C.T.I.O.N


The first Rotaract District 3131 PAO Project- A.C.T.I.O.N: Active Congregation of Talented, Innovative & Organised Networks, A Masterclass on Entrepreneurship has been successfully running for two months has set the motive to infuse the ability and readiness to develop, organize & run an enterprise through innovative ideas and strategies through Rotaractors. It has always given importance to research, the pursuit of knowledge dissemination, and practical thinking, which continues to be the lynchpin of its legacy. The initiative has been attracting participants across Intra-District as well as Inter-District over the globe namely Nepal, Sri Lanka, Bhutan, United Kingdom, Canada, and many more. The sessions have been graced by renowned trainers Rtn Pradeep Wagh, Mr.Santosh Sali, Rtn Surendra Patwardhan, Rtn Dr Kamaljeet Kaur, and many more. With the intension of diffusing the feeling of “I AM AN OWNER” through the time of Pandemic, we are glad to share that the team has received an affirmative reply for a funding opportunity for the Best Business Plans from Solanki Group of Companies based in Canada through the initiative “INDIA-Rotaract-Startup-Funding Program”. We are delighted to create such suitable conditions needed for a dynamic professional world through this masterclass that is conducted every Second Sunday over virtual space and facilitate Rotapreneurs to make their dream come true.


BINOD


A platform created by the Club Service Directors for Rotaract District 3131 and 3170 to come together with their District Council members and Club Service Directors.
The idea was to get to know each other and establish a bond stronger than we already had. It was a difficult task to do it online but then Club Service Directors of RID3131 made it possible with all their innovative and fun ideas. We started with general games like Caption This, Baby Words, and Tongue Twisters where around 90 Rotaracts participated from both the districts. It became more fun when we started nominating each other for tongue twisters. After that, we randomly divided everyone into smaller groups where Council members were divided into two groups and asked to act out an advertisement. While the CSDs were divided into smaller groups where they got to interact with each other and get to know how differently they function.
Once the breakout rooms were dissolved into one single room the Council members presented the funniest ways to sell a product. The event was concluded with a rapid-fire round with Core council members of both the districts where they had to imagine being in weird situations. And then a Review and congratulatory note from both District's side for the organizing team.
